Veeam: Insufficient Disk Space

Real-World Solutions for Engineers

Issue

Backup job fails due to repository running out of space.

Common Causes

  • Retention policy misconfigured
  • Old backups not deleted
  • Capacity tier not configured

Resolution

  1. Check repository free space.
  2. Run repository rescan.
  3. Verify retention settings.
  4. Offload backups to capacity tier (if configured).
